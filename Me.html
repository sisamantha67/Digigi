Ysabella Samantha M. Azura

I, Ysa, an art loving and editor is a junior high school student passionate about technology, leadership, and social awareness. I enjoy creating digital content and advocating for youth empowerment. Through this magazine, I hopes to inspire students to take action in bridging the digital gap in their communities.

The digital divide is one of the most urgent issues in today’s technology-driven world. While some students enjoy high-speed internet, modern devices, and unlimited information, others struggle just to connect. This inequality affects education, job opportunities, healthcare access, and social participation. In this issue, we explore the causes of the digital divide, its impact on students and communities, and the possible solutions that governments, schools, and young people can take to create a more inclusive digital future.

As a student, I have seen how important internet access is for assignments, research, and communication. During online classes, some students were left behind because they had no stable connection or device. This made me realize that technology is not just a convenience—it is a necessity. Raising awareness about the digital divide is the first step toward creating equal opportunities for everyone.
